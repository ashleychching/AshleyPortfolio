Mobile App • Hackathon Project

Pancake Pals

A whimsical mobile game that gamifies productivity through friendly competition and adorable characters. Build your pancake stack, explore a cozy world, and compete with friends in weekly challenges.

Role

Product Designer & Developer

Type

Hackathon Project

Platform

iOS, Android

Tools

React Native, Figma

Overview

Pancake Pals is a social productivity game that transforms goal-setting into an engaging adventure. Through charming character designs and a cozy illustrated world, the app encourages users to build healthy habits while connecting with friends through weekly competitions and collaborative challenges.

What makes Pancake Pals unique is its emphasis on creating a warm, inviting experience that feels more like exploring a storybook than tracking productivity. The hand-drawn art style and food-themed metaphors make habit formation feel approachable and fun rather than overwhelming or clinical.

The Core Experience

At its heart, Pancake Pals revolves around three interconnected features that work together to create motivation, community, and progress tracking:

The World Map

One of the most delightful aspects of Pancake Pals is its illustrated world map that connects all the features together. The map shows a whimsical landscape with winding paths, cozy buildings, and natural scenery rendered in a beautiful hand-drawn style.

This map serves as both navigation and storytelling device, making the app feel like an explorable world rather than just a collection of screens. It reinforces the game-like nature of the experience and gives users a sense of place and progression as they move between different areas.

Pancake-ium: Your Weekly Competition Hub

The main interface displays your current challenge status with an adorable character mascot representing you. Each week presents a new competition theme, and your progress is visualized through a bar chart showing your daily activity over the week. The leaderboard shows how you're ranking against friends, creating friendly competition that motivates without feeling overly competitive or stressful.

The design uses soft, muted colors—sage greens, warm creams, and gentle browns—to create a calming atmosphere. Characters are represented as cute toast, ghost, and strawberry avatars, each with their own personality that players can choose and customize.

Bakery Shop: Achievements & Rewards

The Bakery Shop serves as your achievement gallery where you can view your earned badges and track your accomplishments. The space features a charming character profile with your chosen avatar, plus a map showing the bakery's location in the larger Pancake Pals world.

Achievements are displayed as decorative pancake medallions, creating a satisfying visual collection that grows as you complete more challenges. This system provides tangible rewards for consistent effort and gives players something to work toward beyond just the weekly competitions.

Add Friends: Building Your Community

The social aspect is central to Pancake Pals' success. The Add Friends screen shows your friend group as a list of toastie characters, each with their own personality. You can easily add new friends to your group and see who's already part of your productivity circle.

This feature emphasizes that progress is more enjoyable and sustainable when shared with others. Friends can cheer each other on, share tips, and celebrate wins together, creating accountability through connection rather than pressure.

Design Philosophy

Every design decision in Pancake Pals was made with warmth and accessibility in mind. The art style draws inspiration from cozy games and children's book illustrations, creating an environment that feels safe, welcoming, and free from judgment.

The color palette of soft greens, warm oranges, and creamy neutrals was specifically chosen to evoke feelings of comfort and nostalgia. Typography is friendly and readable, and the interface uses gentle curves and organic shapes rather than harsh geometric forms.

By framing productivity through the lens of a charming game world, Pancake Pals removes the intimidation often associated with habit tracking apps. It's not about perfection—it's about showing up, having fun with friends, and celebrating small wins along the way.

Technical Implementation

Built with React Native during a hackathon timeframe, Pancake Pals required efficient cross-platform development to bring the vision to life quickly. The app integrates with device activity tracking APIs to monitor user progress while maintaining privacy and data security.

The charming illustrated assets were created in Figma and optimized for mobile performance. Animations were kept subtle and purposeful—small movements and transitions that add personality without overwhelming the user or draining battery life.

Impact & Learning

Creating Pancake Pals taught me valuable lessons about designing for motivation and behavior change. The key insight was that productivity apps don't need to be serious or utilitarian—sometimes a playful, story-driven approach can be more effective at helping people stick with their goals.

The social features proved crucial during user testing. Participants reported that seeing their friends on the leaderboard motivated them more than any numerical goal could. The friendly competition created natural accountability while the cute characters kept the mood light and fun.

This project reinforced my belief that thoughtful design can transform mundane tasks into delightful experiences. By wrapping productivity tracking in a warm, whimsical package, Pancake Pals makes building better habits feel less like work and more like play.
