Orthopedic Practice Website (2024)

A fully functional website for a private orthopedic practice, designed to provide patients with easy access to information about services, doctor profiles, and practice locations.

Role

Full Stack Developer & Designer

Duration

2 months

Platform

Web

Tools

Vue.js, Tailwind CSS

The problem

The orthopedic private practice did not have a website to display their information and the services that they provide. They needed a portal where patients could access information in a simple and straightforward manner.

The solution

I designed and coded this project with a friend to make it as informational and digestible as possible. We collectively decided that yellow and blue were good colors for the color palette because these colors have connotations of being trustworthy. Since a medical professional should be trustworthy, we decided to stick with these colors.

Design decisions

We added an image of the doctor on the front screen because when people associate a face with a doctor, it makes them more credible. We placed the "Find a Location" and "Learn More" buttons on the front page because those call-to-actions made the most sense for a patient searching for care.

We also included informational pages for the types of services that the practice provides, so patients could learn more about the symptoms they are experiencing and find the right care for their needs.

Results

Built and launched a complete website for a private orthopedic practice, handling everything from UI/UX design to production. Tested across different browsers and devices to ensure consistent performance on desktop and mobile. Worked directly with the client to define requirements upfront, reducing revision cycles and delivering all requested features. Created reusable components in Vue.js and Tailwind, improving code efficiency and speeding up future development.
