About Harp Research

Harp Research is a company based in Troy, New York that uses machine learning and AI to create products that simplify life. One of their notable products is Viewpoint, which automates email workflows. The company is constantly working to improve their polymorphic AI framework, pushing the boundaries of what's possible with artificial intelligence.

The Problem

Harp Research wanted a redesign of their current website as it was built on Wix and was not customized to their brand. The existing site didn't reflect the innovative and cutting-edge nature of their AI products, and lacked the professional polish expected from a technology company at the forefront of machine learning.

I collaborated with a team to create a comprehensive redesign based on the existing brand guidelines, transforming the site from a generic template into a custom experience that truly represents Harp Research's mission and capabilities.

Landing Page

Before

The original landing page lacked visual hierarchy and failed to communicate the company's core value proposition effectively. The Wix template constraints limited the design possibilities.

After

The redesigned landing page features a clean, modern layout with improved typography and visual hierarchy. The new design immediately communicates Harp Research's expertise in AI and machine learning while guiding visitors toward key information and calls-to-action.

Contact Form and Footer

Before

The original contact form and footer were generic and didn't align with the brand identity. They lacked visual appeal and failed to encourage user engagement.

After

The redesigned contact form features a streamlined interface with clear input fields and improved accessibility. The footer now includes organized navigation, social links, and contact information in a visually balanced layout.

About Page

Before

The original About page struggled to tell Harp Research's story in a compelling way, with cluttered layout and poor content organization.

After

The redesigned About page now tells a cohesive story about Harp Research's mission, vision, and capabilities. The improved layout guides users through the content naturally while highlighting key achievements and products like Viewpoint.

Team Page

Before

The original team page featured a basic grid layout that didn't do justice to the talented individuals behind Harp Research's innovations.

After

The redesigned team page showcases the Harp Research team with improved visual presentation, better photography integration, and clearer information hierarchy for each team member.

New Feature: Open Source Projects

As part of the redesign, we introduced a dedicated section for Harp Research's open source contributions. This new feature highlights the company's commitment to the developer community and showcases their public projects and contributions to the AI/ML ecosystem.

The open source projects section features an organized grid layout with project cards, descriptions, and links to GitHub repositories, making it easy for developers and potential partners to explore Harp Research's public work.

Results & Impact

The redesigned website successfully transformed Harp Research's online presence from a generic Wix template to a custom, brand-aligned digital experience. The new site better communicates the company's expertise in AI and machine learning while providing an improved user experience across all pages.

Key improvements include enhanced visual hierarchy, better content organization, improved mobile responsiveness, and the addition of new features like the open source projects showcase. The redesign positions Harp Research as the innovative technology leader they are.
